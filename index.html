Reward payout

Demo for 15.S12 Applied Behavioral Economics Class

Note: Please use with Chrome or Firefox

Enter your MIT kerberos ID for saving your data later:

Your goal is to maximize the total reward that you get. Your reward payout for each move is shown by the height of the reward bar.

Note that the bar does not reflect your cumulative reward, it shows your reward for each move.

For each move, please click either the red or blue box.

You will play 100 moves in total. At the end of the game, the site will let you save a .csv file that you should upload to Canvas

Good luck!
